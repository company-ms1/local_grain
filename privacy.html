Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how RentTRX collects, uses, and protects information when you use our TRON Energy rental service.

2. Information We Collect

  • Blockchain Data: Wallet addresses, transaction hashes, and amounts.
  • Contact Information: Provided voluntarily, such as email or Telegram handle when contacting support.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and basic analytics for site functionality.

3. How We Use Information

  • To process your TRX Energy purchase and deliver the service.
  • To provide customer support when requested.
  • To monitor service performance and prevent abuse.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Information may be shared only if:

  • Required by law or legal process.
  • Necessary to detect and prevent fraudulent or abusive activity.

5. Data Retention

We retain blockchain transaction data indefinitely (as it is public) and store any support correspondence for up to 12 months.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to secure stored information, but we cannot guarantee 100% protection against unauthorized access.
